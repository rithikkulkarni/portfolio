Overview

My first summer as a Research Intern at 3D Systems introduced me to the forefront of 3D bio-printing innovation within one of the leading companies in the 3D printing industry. Tasked with exploring advancements in bio-printing technologies, I researched emerging solutions and applications that could redefine healthcare. This earned me intimate knowledge of the cutting-edge developments in bio-printing and allowed me to contribute to 3D Systems' R&D efforts.

The first few days were a deep dive into the field of bio-printing, as I familiarized myself with the specific technologies, materials, and methodologies central to the field. I researched industry breakthroughs and reviewed recent developments, gaining a fundamental understanding of the state of bio-printing and the extent of its capabilities. This initial research phase helped me align my project goals with the practical needs and strategic objectives of the R&D team.

My primary responsibility was to create a comprehensive "state-of-the-solutions" report for the team. This report addressed broad industry questions as well as specific inquiries related to emerging products and techniques. I compiled detailed analyses of existing and potential applications, such as tissue scaffolding, organ prototypes, and bio-material compatibility. With these findings, I created a resource that offered both high-level insights and technical details to guide the team in future project decisions.

This internship honed my skills in technical research, report writing, and analytical thinking. Diving deep into understanding my research sharpened my ability to synthesize vast amounts of information into concise insights. Crafting the final report aided my technical writing skills, as I learned to communicate findings clearly and concisely for a specialized audience. Additionally, exploring bio-printing applications in healthcare enhanced my understanding of how innovative technologies can be leveraged to address real-world problems.

Reflecting on my time at 3D Systems, I realize how impactful this internship was in shaping my understanding of research and development. This experience exposed me to the potential to revolutionize medical research and healthcare. Contributing to a project with far-reaching implications solidified my interest in technology.
