Overview

My summer internship at Axtra3D offered a unique opportunity to work with a company transitioning from its startup phase to an established presence in the 3D printing industry. As a relatively new company founded in Italy, Axtra3D has quickly gained recognition with its flagship product, the Lumia X1. This experience gave me a hands-on introduction to the world of 3D printing and data analytics, allowing me to explore both the technical and strategic sides of industry.

During my first month, I immersed myself in Axtra’s lab environment, where I quickly learned the company’s 3D printing processes and material testing protocols. My initial role involved conducting material benchmarking and printer performance testing for the Lumia X1, helping prospective customers evaluate the printer’s capabilities. This work required fine-tuning design files, printing requested parts, and reporting my findings to customers to showcase the Lumia X1’s quality and efficiency. Interacting directly with the product and receiving honest feedback from prospective customers helped me provide Axtra with valuable insights and revealed areas for improvement in the printer’s functionality.

With a foundational understanding of Axtra3D’s technology and customer needs, I transitioned to perform data analysis that supported the company’s strategic goals. Collaborating closely with the Chief Strategy Officer, I analyzed customer feedback data, marketing performance history, and buyer relations. My work contributed to ongoing marketing strategies, and I gained experience in representing data in a way that could support predictive modeling and targeted marketing efforts. Gaining exposure to both technical work in the lab and analytical responsibilities in the office, I gained a unique perspective on the synergy between product performance and customer perception.

Through this internship, I developed essential skills in 3D printing technology, data analysis, and customer relations. My time in the lab reinforced the importance of precision and adaptability in managing tasks, while my data work introduced me to the basics of data science and its impact on strategic decision-making. Learning to visualize and interpret data insights honed my analytical skills, and my work with the Chief Strategy Officer taught me how data-driven strategies can shape a company’s market approach and customer engagement.

Reflecting on my internship at Axtra3D, I realize how valuable this experience was in expanding my understanding of the inner workings of tech companies. Being part of a fast-growing company allowed me to see firsthand how innovation and strategy intersect to drive success. My exposure to both the technical and strategic sides of Axtra’s operations deepened my appreciation for data-driven decision-making and further fueled my interest in the applications of emerging technologies.


Key Skills

3D Printing/Material Benchmarking: Conducted detailed performance testing and material benchmarking for the Lumia X1 printer, polishing design files and printing parts to demonstrate product quality and efficiency for prospective customers.
Data Analysis/Visualization: Analyzed customer feedback, marketing performance, and buyer relations data to inform marketing strategies, gaining practical experience in data representation and predictive modeling within a data science context.
Data-Driven Marketing: Worked closely with the Chief Strategy Officer to apply data insights in strategic marketing decisions, developing skills in corporate strategy, customer relations, and data-driven decision-making to enhance overall product and market positioning.
