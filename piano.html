Hi! I'm Rithik Kulkarni, a local piano teacher and computer science undergraduate at NC State University.

I've been playing piano for 16 years, and I love sharing my passion for music with others. I have experience teaching students of all ages and levels, and I aim to inspire them to develop disciplined practice habits and a love for music. My journey with piano has been a rewarding one, and I hope to help others discover the joy of playing as well. For all students' varying musical interests, I believe there is something for everyone with the piano. I am currently accepting new students for in-person lessons in the Raleigh area. In addition to teaching, I also create content on YouTube, where I share performances and my own arrangements of popular music. Feel free to reach out via email or text if you are interested in lessons or have any questions!
