About Me

Hello! I'm a BSCS student at NC State University concentrating in Artificial Intelligence. During my undergrad, I am focusing on Artificial Intelligence and its widespread use in today's world.

I'm interested in the data science tools and techniques utilized to fuel AI systems. I enjoy and have experience in full-stack product management and development, as showcased via various projects and internships. These interests have led me to pursue a career as a software engineer focused on Machine Learning.

In addition to my passions for Software Engineering and AI, I have several hobbies that play a major role in the person I strive to be.

Most notably, I serve as President of Model United Nations at NC State, a role I devote much time to. As President, I got NC State's Model UN team off its feet by mentoring its population of aspiring diplomats, facilitating the beginning of a new partnership with UNC-Chapel Hill's Carolina International Relations Association, and leading the team at conferences nationwide.

In my free time, I perform and create instructive content to help people learn the piano on YouTube. This has honed my skills in trend analysis and adaptation to user feedback--skills that have helped me reach over 1 million viewers creating online content.

I grew up in Charlotte, North Carolina and developed an interest in learning, soon finding my calling as a leader and a pianist. Over the years, I've enjoyed exploring various other pursuits, from my experience as an eSports competitor to my accomplishments as an intermediate chess player.
